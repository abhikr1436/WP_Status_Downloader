# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 17, 2026**

This privacy policy applies to the **WP Status Downloader** app (herein referred to as "Application") for mobile devices, created by **Olfoxacin**.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

The Application is designed to help users download and save statuses from WhatsApp. 

### a. Personal Information
The Application does not require you to provide any personally identifiable information (PII) to use its core features. We do not collect, store, or share your name, email address, phone number, or contacts on any remote servers.

### b. Media and Files
To function correctly, the Application requires access to your device's storage (or specific media folders) to read WhatsApp status files and save them to your gallery. 
- The Application reads media files from the WhatsApp status folder locally on your device.
- It saves selected files to your device's public "Pictures/StatusSaver" folder.
- **We do not upload your media files to any external servers.**

### c. Automatically Collected Information
The Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to:
- The type of mobile device you use.
- Your mobile device's unique device ID.
- The IP address of your mobile device.
- Your mobile operating system.
- Information about the way you use the Application.

## 2. Third-Party Services

The Application uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These include:

*   **Google Play Services**: Standard services for app functionality on Android.
*   **Google AdMob**: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data (such as advertising IDs) to show personalized ads.
    *   [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
    *   [How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)

## 3. Permissions

The Application requests the following permissions:
- **Storage/Media Access**: To read and write status files.
- **Internet**: To load advertisements and provide feedback features.

## 4. Data Retention Policy

Since we do not collect personal data on our own servers, we do not retain any of your personal information. Any media saved by the Application remains on your local device until you delete it.

## 5. Children's Privacy

The Application does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our records.

## 6. Security

We value your trust in providing us access to your media folders for the purpose of the app's functionality. We strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it, but remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
